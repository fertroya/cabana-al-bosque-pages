Club de Campo Dos Valles

Private neighborhood with 24-hour perimeter security, 15 minutes from downtown Bariloche. Tennis, soccer, and rugby courts, pool, gym, over 30 km of trails for trekking and mountain biking, and green spaces among pine forest with mountain views.

Explore the club facilities →

SPA Dos Valles

The club's wellness center is at the Club House: indoor-outdoor heated pool, gym with mountain views, jacuzzi, sauna, and relaxation lounge. There are also yoga, swimming, and functional training classes (check schedules and reservations with the neighborhood administration).

La Salamandra Pulpería

Countryside restaurant within the neighborhood, at Acceso al Challhuaco: Patagonian cuisine in a dining room with stone and wood fireplace. Ideal for a special dinner without leaving the country club. Advance reservation required to coordinate access.

Google Maps · Instagram · Reservations via WhatsApp

Belek Food Truck

Craft beer and burgers inside the country club: Belek's food truck at Dos Valles is perfect for dinner or drinks without driving into town. Check seasonal hours on their Instagram.
