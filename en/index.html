Before you book

Straightforward answers about the cabin, the neighborhood, and how to plan your stay at Dos Valles.

We are inside Club de Campo Dos Valles, on the outskirts of San Carlos de Bariloche. Access is via a paved road; it's about 15–20 minutes by car from downtown. We send precise directions when your reservation is confirmed.

Dos Valles has 24-hour perimeter security, controlled access, and plenty of green space among the pines. The cabin is independent: you have your own patio and grill, but you can also use the club facilities (pool, gym, courts, and trails). It's ideal if you want tranquility without feeling isolated.

Two bedrooms with three beds and a recommended capacity of up to 5 guests. Works very well for families or two couples. If you're more, message us on WhatsApp and we'll see case by case.

Kitchen with refrigerator, oven, and complete tableware; linens and towels; WiFi; Smart TV; wood-burning fireplace in the living room; on-site parking and outdoor grill. The idea is that you arrive, settle in, and start enjoying.

Yes, as a guest you have access to the country club amenities: heated pool, gym, tennis, soccer, and rugby courts, over 30 km of trails, and the SPA Dos Valles. Hours and activity reservations are arranged with the neighborhood administration.

Inside the neighborhood is La Salamandra Pulpería, a countryside restaurant with Patagonian cuisine (advance reservation required). In summer and winter the club usually has food services at the Club House; check what's active for your dates when you arrive.

Yes. There is stable WiFi and a comfortable desk nook. Many guests combine remote work with walks in the forest or an afternoon at the neighborhood pool.

Winter: we're about 20 minutes from Cerro Catedral; a car with proper snow tires is recommended. Summer: lakes, beaches, and trekking just minutes away. In both seasons the forest surroundings and country club amenities add a lot to the experience.

In Bariloche and Dos Valles, having your own wheels makes a big difference. If you don't have a car yet, message us with your stay dates and we'll quote a rental through a trusted local agency — no obligation.

Yes. For stays of more than 5 nights, we can arrange a simple initial grocery shop — drinks, breakfast basics, and essentials — so you arrive with the fridge and pantry ready. Message us on WhatsApp in advance and we'll put the list together with you.

You can book directly with us on WhatsApp, or via Booking.com and Airbnb. Standard check-in from 3:00 PM and check-out by 10:00 AM. If your flight or bus doesn't match, let us know in advance and we'll see if we can accommodate your schedule.
